# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a respectful and harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in discussions
- Focus on the topic and provide helpful feedback
- Accept constructive criticism gracefully

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Spam or off-topic content
- Publishing others' private information

## Enforcement

Violations may result in removal of comments, issues, or blocking from the repository. Reports can be sent to **security@griffin31.ai**.
